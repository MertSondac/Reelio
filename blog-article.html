Sometimes its not about you

Pharra Perry Account Manager at Reelio Labs & Internet Junkie

So this is making the rounds and we need to talk about it. Youtuber Casey Neistat just gave “The Secret Life of Walter Mitty” the best promotion it could’ve asked for. Here’s what Neistat did. Grab some cocoa, watch it and we’ll analyze why this was brilliant.

What are the top 3 things you will walk away remembering about this video? - Movie studio reached out to a guy asking him to promote their movie - Guy spends $25,000 on relief in the Philippines instead - A new Ben Stiller movie is out (bonus points if you remember the title) Great magicians are known for mastering the art of misdirection— make them watch this hand to distract from what’s happening with the other hand.
